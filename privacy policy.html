A persistent cookie will be reactivated when a user returns to the website which posted the cookie. We and our third-party advertisers and service providers use Cookies to collect information in order to facilitate access to the App and Service and to improve our App and Service for you. The Cookies make your use of our App and Service easier, make the App and Service run more smoothly and help us to maintain a secure App and Service. Most internet browsers automatically accept Cookies, but you can instruct your browser, by editing its options, to refuse all Cookies or to indicate when a Cookie is being sent. The “Help” feature on most browsers provides information on how to accept Cookies, disable Cookies or to notify you when receiving a new Cookie. If you do not accept Cookies, you may not be able to use some features of our App and Service. We recommend that you leave the Cookies turned on when using our App and Service.
